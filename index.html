01 Executive Summary

A differentiated concept,
built on disciplined economics.

Pixel World is a proposed retro-arcade theme park in Dubai, built on a single distinctive idea: every attraction is a level, and every visitor is a player. It pairs the universal nostalgia of classic gaming with the production values and safety standards the region's leisure market now expects — fully indoor, climate-controlled and gamified end-to-end.

Pixel World is seeking AED 750 million to complete the design, construction and pre-launch of the park, in exchange for a 40% equity stake. The opportunity rests on three foundations — a large and growing visitor market, a product with no direct regional competitor, and a diversified revenue model that does not depend on ticket sales alone.

USP

The only park where the visit itself is a game.

Every guest wears a Pixel Band that scores every ride, unlocks levels and ranks families on a live leaderboard. No park in the region — indoor or outdoor — offers a gamified end-to-end visit. That single mechanic drives repeat visits (beat your score), longer stays (one more level) and premium spend (power-ups, passes, merch).

02 Why Invest
