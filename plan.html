07 Risks & Mitigations

We've stress-tested the downside.

Every major risk has a concrete mitigation already built into the plan — not bolted on afterwards.

Risk 01 · Competitive

Established competition

IMG, Motiongate and LEGOLAND have scale and brand recognition.

Mitigation: We don't compete head-on — no Dubai park is gaming-themed, gamified or leaderboard-driven. A category of one.
Risk 02 · Capital

High build cost

A AED 750m project carries capital and timeline exposure.

Mitigation: Phased openings pull revenue forward, a 4% contingency reserve absorbs overruns, fixed-price contracts cap downside.
Risk 03 · Seasonal

Low summer footfall

Dubai's summer heat suppresses outdoor attraction demand.

Mitigation: 100% indoor and climate-controlled — directly serving the 59% who prefer indoor venues in summer. Our weather risk becomes our advantage.
Risk 04 · Macro

Economic downturn

Discretionary spend falls when the economy softens.

Mitigation: Tiered pricing keeps an accessible entry point; season passes lock in recurring revenue; 40%+ of income is non-ticket.
